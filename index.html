Molleindustria: the macdonalds videogame

The video game the macdonalds videogame by artist group Molleindustria is a simulator game where the player operates the MacDonalds franchise through management of purchases and segments of internal operations. The game is endless, the only ending to the game is if the player’s operations become too costly and the simulated MacDonalds corporation goes bankrupt, causing the player to be fired. In the game, the player controls four individual scenes of operations that are interconnected; for example, even if the player has managed to set up a balanced agricultural sector, having an ineKective advertising campaign may lower traKic to MacDonald’s restaurants, causing the uncovered operation costs of the agricultural sector to bankrupt the player. The game’s many systems all promote the idea of balance, the player must operate the sectors as components of one operation rather than individual operations. The game is playable in the browser and also downloadable on Windows OS and Mac OS.
